# 台灣第一家烏龍嗨 · 空間示意圖 Prompts

> 給 AI 生圖工具用的提示詞集（Midjourney / Flux / Nano Banana / DALL-E 通用）。
> 風格定位：**乾淨昭和感的台北午後酒場**——新而不亮、舊而不髒、有歲月但被好好照顧。
> 重點：**空間「氣味」的編輯影像**，不是室內設計圖、不是 3D render。

---

## 品牌空間 DNA（所有 prompt 共通的視覺語彙）

| 維度 | 是 | 不是 |
|---|---|---|
| **色溫** | 3000K 鎢絲燈、暖琥珀、午後斜陽 | 冷白 LED、霓虹、舞台聚光 |
| **材質** | 暖橡木、米色亞麻、和紙、銅、磨砂玻璃、皮革 | 黑色大理石、鏡面、不鏽鋼、混凝土 |
| **配色** | 米白 / 茶褐 / 焙茶橙 / 麥色 / 墨色 | 高飽和、純黑純白、撞色 |
| **參考時代** | 90 年代日式喫茶店 + 下町居酒屋 + 老派唱片行 | speakeasy、hotel bar、IG 網美店 |
| **狀態** | well-maintained patina · 新而不亮、舊而不髒、被日常擦拭過、淡淡使用痕跡 | 全新樣品屋、刻意做舊、骯髒老店、antique 古物店、廢墟感 |
| **情緒** | 慢、安靜、午後、可獨處可結伴 | 派對、熱鬧、打卡 |

## 通用樣式後綴（每個 prompt 結尾接這段）

```
Editorial photography, clean Showa-inspired Taipei afternoon bar,
new but not shiny, aged but not dirty, well-maintained patina,
warm tungsten 3000K lighting, soft amber reflections, cream / tea-brown /
hojicha / warm oak palette, lived-in but clean, cared-for not distressed,
subtle 35mm film grain, quiet contemporary Taiwanese-Japanese sensibility,
Kinfolk × Apartamento × Hirokazu Kore-eda mood,
no chrome, no marble, no neon, no LED. Aspect ratio 3:2.
```

> **關鍵 patina 用語**：寫具體場景時可加入這些精準形容詞——
> `well-maintained patina` / `clean worn wood` / `lightly used leather` /
> `quietly polished by daily use` / `not distressed, not antique`。

## 通用負面詞（Negative prompt）

```
dirty old shop, dusty abandoned interior, antique store, over-aged wood,
grimy walls, stained ceiling, cluttered junk, heavy sepia filter,
yellowed vintage photo, excessive nostalgia, broken furniture,
dark tavern, smoky bar, black marble, polished chrome, mirror wall,
industrial concrete, cold blue LED, fluorescent ceiling, neon sign,
speakeasy hidden door, hotel lobby bar, all-white minimal, IKEA showroom,
fake plants, people posing for camera, hyperreal 3D render,
architectural visualization, HDR, oversaturated.
```

## 12 個鏡頭

### 01 · 吧台主視覺（Hero shot — 進門第一眼）

**意圖**：客人推開門看到的第一個畫面。整個空間的氣味立刻傳達——溫暖、低調、有光澤。

```
Editorial photograph of a warm, lived-in afternoon shochu bar in Taipei,
inspired by 90s Showa kissaten and Tokyo shitamachi izakaya.
A long warm-oak bar counter occupies the right two-thirds of the frame,
with five tan-leather cushioned stools, slightly worn at the seats.
Behind the bar: a low amber-lit shelf with about 15 shochu bottles
(handwritten kraft-paper labels), ceramic tea jars, and three vinyl records
leaning against the wall. Soft tungsten pendant lights cast honey-colored
pools of light onto the counter. A potted small olive plant on the far end.
Cream hand-folded paper menus on the counter. Dust motes in slanted afternoon
sunlight coming through frosted-glass windows on the left.
[+ 通用樣式後綴]
```

### 02 · 客人視角（從吧台座位看 bartender）

**意圖**：客人坐下後的視角。低調的工藝感、人與酒之間的安靜。

```
First-person view from a bar stool looking across a worn warm-oak counter
at a calm bartender in his mid-30s wearing a simple beige linen apron,
slowly pouring shochu over a single large clear ice cube into a faceted
Edo Kiriko crystal glass. Behind him: a wall of small wooden tea drawers
labeled in faded Japanese characters (茶, 麦, 焙), a low shelf of shochu
bottles backlit with amber light, a small framed handwritten calligraphy.
On the counter foreground: a folded cream paper napkin, a hand-drawn
menu card, a small ceramic dish with pickled ginger.
Late afternoon tungsten light, soft shadows, no flash.
Documentary editorial photography, lived-in not staged.
[+ 通用樣式後綴]
```

### 03 · 角落二人座 / 慢飲座位

### 04 · 入口立面（從巷子裡看店面）

slightly wet from earlier rain, reflecting the warm window light.
No neon, no fluorescent, no glossy hotel-bar finish.
Aged but cared for. Wong Kar-wai golden-hour mood, editorial street photo.

### 07 · 吧台後酒櫃 / 選物牆

**意圖**：「我們不堆酒牆」的具體視覺——量不多但每瓶都有理由。

```
Editorial mid-shot of the wall directly behind a small Taipei shochu bar.
Cream-painted wooden shelves, hand-rubbed not glossy, holding about 30
shochu bottles total—organized in four loose clusters by family
(mugi 麦, imo 芋, kokuto 黒糖, kome 米), with deliberate empty space
between clusters to breathe. Each bottle has a small hand-written
kraft-paper neck label in Chinese and Japanese. Two of the shelves are
not for bottles—one holds a row of small wooden tea drawers with brass
pulls labeled in faded calligraphy, another holds a small vintage Pioneer
turntable and ten vinyl record spines. A single warm picture light at
the top throws amber down across the bottles. The shelving feels
considered and human-sized, not a hotel-bar trophy wall. A small
handwritten sign on the wall reads '我們不賣酒，賣選擇。'
[+ 通用樣式後綴]
```

### 08 · 店主背影 / 主理人

**意圖**：「朋友來我家分享有趣的東西」——主理人是空間的靈魂，但不是被擺拍的對象。

```
Documentary editorial photograph from behind, three-quarter angle.
A calm man in his mid-30s in a simple beige linen apron and rolled-up
shirt sleeves, standing behind the bar slicing a piece of citrus on a
worn wooden cutting board, his back and shoulders the main subject.
We do not see his face directly—just the curve of his jaw and the focus
of his hands. Late afternoon tungsten light from the side, soft shadow.
A folded white cloth on his shoulder, a small pencil tucked behind his ear.
Behind him: shelves of shochu bottles slightly out of focus.
Foreground: the empty bar counter from the customer's side, a single
folded menu, an empty Edo Kiriko glass waiting.
The mood is "a friend at home making something for you," not
"a bartender performing." Hirokazu Kore-eda domestic warmth,
natural existing light, no posing, no eye contact with camera.
[+ 通用樣式後綴]
```

### 11 · 洗手間 / 細節考究

**意圖**：細節哲學的試金石——一家店是不是真的講究，看廁所就知道。

```
Editorial photograph of a small bathroom in a Taipei afternoon shochu bar.
Cream small-format square tiles on the lower wall (slightly imperfect,
hand-laid grout lines), warm walnut wood paneling above.
A simple round mirror in a thin unlacquered brass frame, slightly fogged
at the edges. A vintage white porcelain wall-mounted basin with a single
brass tap, tea-stained where water has run for years.
On a small wooden ledge beside the basin: a handmade ceramic dish
holding a single bar of cream-colored hinoki soap, a small folded
cream linen hand towel, a tiny ceramic vase with a single sprig of
dried wheat. A single warm tungsten wall sconce in unlacquered brass
casting honey light. The floor is unpolished terrazzo with small
warm-colored chips.
A small framed print on the wall: faded kanji calligraphy reading '清'
on cream washi paper, in a thin walnut frame.
Quiet, intimate, considered—not luxurious, not industrial,
no chrome, no marble, no hotel finish, no glass shower stall.
The kind of bathroom that proves the rest of the bar's craftsmanship.
[+ 通用樣式後綴]
```

### 12 · 一張被照顧過的吧台（乾淨昭和感 essence）

**意圖**：把品牌的「**新而不亮、舊而不髒、有歲月但被好好照顧**」濃縮成一張——空無一人、只有一張剛被擦過的木吧台。是這個風格的 essence shot，可以當作風格基準。

```
Close-up editorial photograph of a long warm-oak bar counter at
late morning around 11 AM, just before opening, no people present.
The wood surface catches soft amber sunlight from a half-frosted
side window — showing a quietly polished patina from years of daily
use, but no stains, no rings, no dust. A folded clean cream linen
cloth rests in the foreground, slightly damp at the edge from a
recent wipe-down. Behind, slightly out of focus: a row of upturned
freshly-polished Edo Kiriko glasses on a wooden tray, a small
ceramic dish, brass ice tongs neatly placed on a folded napkin.
The space reads new but not shiny, aged but not dirty,
well-maintained patina, quietly polished by daily use.
No clutter, no distress, no antique-shop dustiness.
[+ 通用樣式後綴]
```

## 用法建議

### 工具差異

| 工具 | 建議 |
|---|---|
| **Midjourney v6+** | 直接貼整段 prompt + `--ar 3:2 --style raw`，先 V4 一輪挑構圖再 upscale |
| **Flux / Nano Banana** | 整段貼，可以再加「`composition: editorial photo, rule of thirds`」 |
| **DALL-E 3 / Sora** | 把 prompt 縮短到 200 字內，移除過多形容詞，留下關鍵物件 + 風格 |
| **Stable Diffusion** | 用通用負面詞、加 LoRA：`Kodak Portra 400` / `editorial film` |

### 套組建議

如果要產一整套品牌視覺，建議：
1. 先生 **#12 一張被照顧過的吧台**（essence shot · 風格基準）
2. 接著生 **#01 吧台主視覺 + #04 入口立面**（最具代表性的兩張）
3. 用同一個 seed / style ref 接著生其他 9 張（保持氣味一致）
4. **不要每張都換風格描述**——同樣的後綴，整套才會像一家店

### 跟現有 7 張 poster 的關係

- Posters（#1–#7）= **品牌符號 / 概念海報**（拼貼、印刷感、抽象）
- 空間示意圖 = **品牌世界觀 / 場景現實**（編輯攝影、具象、可被想像走進去）

兩者一起，等於有了「概念面」+「現實面」的完整品牌視覺資產。

## 想試其他鏡頭？

延伸方向（還沒寫的）：
- **下班時段的窗外**（玻璃內外光線對撞、可放在「下午酒場」概念上）
- **菜單被翻開的瞬間**（連結 #6 海報但是現實版）
- **雨天的店面 / 內部**（換天氣換氣味、不一樣的午後）
- **季節限定 / 4 季 4 張**（春櫻 / 夏抹茶 / 秋焙茶 / 冬黑糖）
- **一杯酒從滿到空**（同個座位、同個杯、4–6 張，講「慢慢喝」）
- **客人視角看桌上**（不是俯拍、不是側拍，是「我自己在喝」的眼睛角度）
- **吧台前的硬幣 / 收銀機 / 結帳細節**（有歲月的零錢盤、手寫帳本）
- **後巷 / 倒垃圾的瞬間**（最反 IG 的 mood 反而會讓人覺得是真的店）

跟我說想要哪個，我用同樣的格式幫你寫。
